Top 10 Male Models to watch in 2022

With the Male model industry growing more and more every day… it is so difficult to know who the newcomers on the scene are! Don’t you worry though, we have you sorted. We have compiled a list of our Top 10 Male Models to watch out for in 2022.

Jacob Hankin

Image: Pinterest

Standing tall at 6’2″, Jacob easily gets the attention of casting directors. Known for his chiselled abs, piercing blue eyes & unique beauty mark – Jacob’s portfolio includes many editorial shoots. He’s starred in spreads for magazines like Hercules, GQ Japan, and GQ Australia. Doing catalog and lookbook work, Jacob has connected with clients such as Harrods, East Dane, and Reiss.

Instagram:
@jacobhankin

Image: Pinterest

Oliver Cheshire

Image: Pinterest

Oliver Cheshire

Oliver has modeled for world-famous brands throughout his career, such as Dolce & Gabbana, Versace, Orlebar Brown, Stella McCartney, Missoni, Hackett, Abercrombie & Fitch, and he is known for always bringing on an increase of 50% in sales when he fronts a campaign.

Instagram:

@oliver_cheshire

Image: Pinterest

Fabian Arnold

Image: Pinterest

Fabian is a sports enthusiast and has been playing soccer and handball for years. That as well as many years of work at the gym (that explains his body). He is ranked on the list of most popular Models in the world, also ranked in the elite list of celebrities born in Germany. Fabian Arnold lived in Sydney Australia for a year. After that experience he said he had “discover(ed) what freedom means”. We wish Fabian would come back down under and explore more of Australian freedom.

Instagram:

@fabianxarnold

Image: Pinterest

Simon Nessman

Image: Pinterest

The Canadian model will be shooting an ad campaign for Givenchy in New York in one day & scaling the north face of a mountain in his native British Columbia, the next. Simon is also engaged in ecological research and education, definitely a name to remember in 2022.

Instagram:

@simon.nessman

Image: Pinterest

Jon Kortajarena

Image: Pinterest

Considered as a ‘style king’ and all-rounder menswear legend. Jon splits his time between fronting campaigns for high-end fashion and high street brands alike. Definitely a name to look for on Instagram as he has been in some of the most stylish shoots you will ever see.

He often says that he would be a photographer if he hadn’t gotten involved in modeling. With that, you can see how passionate he is about high fashion.

Instagram:

@jonjortajarena

Image: Pinterest

Luka Sabbat

Image: Pinterest

Member of the new generation of models who are proudly active on social media, Luka Sabbat is one of the most impressive male models of this generation. At only 19 years old and already with a fantastic portfolio working with the likes of D&G and Hugo Boss – he has also helped to showcase several seasons of the Kanye West YEEZY range. His modelling talent, coupled with his social media savviness, creates the perfect storm – giving rise to one of the most influential figures in fashion today.

Instagram:

@lukasabbat

Image: Pinterest

Kit Butler

Image: Pinterest

This is a name that you will be seeing a lot in years to come, with an ever-expanding CV and a large number of followers on social media. Kit was spotted by a retired model who suggested he should go in for an agency meeting. He was signed on the spot and found himself walking shows for Burberry, Armani and Stone Island within months, to name just a few.

Instagram:

@kitbutler

Image: Pinterest

Hu Bing

Image: Pinterest

Hu was forced to drop out of his sport due to an injury at the age of 19. Soon after, his modeling career took off, and it wasn’t long before he was being booked by Valentino and Louis Vuitton. Today he’s one of the most respected names in male modeling and a decorated industry veteran.

Instagram:

@hubing

Image: Pinterest

Marlon Teixeira

Image: Pinterest

This 28 year old Brazillian model is known for his chiselled bone structure and cast iron abs, a result of lifetime surfing back to Brazil. His career began when his grandmother introduced him to Anderson Baumgartner, owner of Way Model Management and friend of the family. A short while later, he was appearing in high-fashion editorials for Vogue, GQ, and Vanity Fair.

Instagram:

@marlontx

Image: Pinsterest

Shaun Ross

Image: Pinterest

After a hard childhood, fighting against bullying, Shaun finally found his niche in the fashion industry. The first-ever albino model, Ross has been involved in photo editorial for GQ, I-D, Vogue and Paper. He has also modelled for Givenchy and Alexander McQueen. If you’ve been paying attention over the past few years, you’ll have noticed runways becoming increasingly diverse, and this determined young model has been at the forefront of it all.

Instagram:

@shaunross
